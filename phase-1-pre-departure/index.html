Phase 1: Pre-Departure Preparation

7-8 Weeks Before Departure

Essential tasks before leaving the US including travel planning, document gathering, medical records, possessions management, and community research.

Essential Travel Planning

  • Buy flight
  • Book temporary accommodation for first 2 weeks
  • Arrange travel health insurance

Get Rid of Things

  • Sell/donate/pack possessions
  • Sell/store car

Gather Documents

  • US medical records (surgeries, MRI, X-rays, etc.)
  • Passport
  • Emergency contact list (doctor, hospital, Jon, US family)
Note: Medical records are essential for DTV application later. This is your last chance to get proper documentation from US doctors.

Research & Planning

  • Join Chiang Mai Expats Facebook groups
  • Understand specific responsibilities of a Financial Sponsor
  • Research prescription medication availability in Thailand
  • Create Thailand budget based on actual costs vs US disability income
  • Identify 24/7 English-speaking medical facilities
  • Plan for burning season (potential southern Thailand/Bali trip during worst air quality months)

Locate Nearby Amenities in Suthep Area

  • Convenience store
  • Grocery store
  • Restaurants
  • AA Meetings
  • Counseling centers, Art Therapy, etc.
  • Gym and swimming pool
  • Physical Therapist
  • Hospital
Info: Suthep area has a large expat community with English-speaking services. Many US medications are available in Thailand at a fraction of US cost.

Key Information

  • Visa: US citizens do NOT need to apply for a visa - you'll receive a 60-day visa exemption automatically upon arrival
  • Medical Records: Essential for DTV later - bring originals and copies
  • Medications: Many US medications available in Thailand at fraction of cost
  • Suthep Area: Large expat community with English-speaking services available
