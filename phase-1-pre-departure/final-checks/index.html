Important Information to Remember

  • Medication Availability: Most US medications are available in Thailand at 25-75% of US prices and often without prescription. Bangkok Hospital in Chiang Mai stocks many Western pharmaceuticals.
  • Medical Records Essential: These documents will be critical foundation for your DTV visa application medical letter. Keep originals in a separate safe place.
  • 90-Day Supply: While not strictly required, carrying a 90-day backup supply provides peace of mind during your transition period and first medical consultations in Thailand.
  • Doctor Recommendations: Any written documentation from your US doctors supporting your need for treatment in Thailand will strengthen your DTV application.
  • Shipping Option: Consider whether to ship any items separately or carry everything in luggage. Medical records can be lightweight if scanned/photographed.
