Thai SIM Cards

Available at airport upon arrival. Major providers: AIS, DTAC, TrueMove. Get set up immediately for local calls and data.

ATM & Banking

ATM Fees: 220 THB (~$6.50) per withdrawal from foreign ATMs. Consider opening a Thai bank account after arrival (Bangkok Bank, Krung Thai, etc.)

Financial & Visa Requirements

DTV Financial Requirements

Minimum Balance: 500,000 THB (~$15,000 USD)
Duration: Must maintain balance for 3+ months
Currency: Can be in Thai or US bank account

Financial Sponsor Documentation

Required:
- Bank statements showing 500,000+ THB for 3+ months
- Sponsorship letter committing to emergency financial support
- Proof of relationship (birth certificate, etc.)

DTV Application Fee

Cost: $340 USD (non-refundable)
Payment: Online during application at thaievisa.go.th

Visa Extension Fee

30-Day Extension: 1,900 THB (~$55)
6-Month Extension: ~$300
Applied at Thai Immigration office
