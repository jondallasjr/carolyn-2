Phase 3: Getting a Medical Plan

Required Foundation for DTV Visa Approval

Sept 16 - Oct 14, 2025 ~4 weeks

This phase is critical: you must establish a formal medical treatment plan with a Thai healthcare provider. This becomes the foundation for your DTV visa application and proves you have legitimate medical reasons for staying in Thailand long-term.

Phase Timeline

Sept 16

Start Medical Plan Process

Begin coordinating with Thai healthcare providers to establish your treatment plan.

Sept 16 - Oct 14

Consultation & Documentation

Complete consultations, gather medical records, and work with providers on official medical letters.

Oct 14

Documentation Complete

Have all medical plans and letters ready for DTV application package.

Key Actions

1. Contact Bangkok Hospital Chiang Mai

Why: Bangkok Hospital has a proven track record of providing strong medical letters for DTV visa approvals.

  • English-speaking staff with international hospital standards
  • Familiar with DTV visa requirements
  • Can coordinate with your physical therapist

2. Schedule Medical Consultation

Expected Cost: $40-90 for initial consultation

Bring to Appointment:

  • US medical records (surgeries, MRI scans, X-rays)
  • Current pain management and treatment needs
  • Physical therapy requirements
  • Any specialist recommendations from US doctors

3. Review Medical Plan with Thai Doctor

Objectives:

  • Doctor reviews your US medical records
  • Establishes ongoing treatment plan in Thailand
  • Documents that 6+ months of treatment is medically necessary
  • Provides cost comparison (PT in US vs Thailand)

4. Secure Medical Letters & Plan

Required Documents:

  • Official medical plan for 6+ months of treatment
  • Doctor's letter confirming medical necessity
  • Treatment schedule and estimated costs
  • Hospital letterhead with contact information

Critical: These documents must be error-free. Typos or inconsistencies cause automatic visa rejection.

Cost Breakdown for This Phase

Bangkok Hospital Consultation $40-90
Physical Therapy Sessions (estimate for 6 months) $150-450
Medical Records Translation (if needed) $50-150
Phase 3 Total Estimate $240-690

Note: Physical therapy in Thailand ($25-75/session) is significantly cheaper than US rates ($100+/session), which strengthens your DTV justification.

  • Active treatment period
